Raid Offline Timer
-by Bubzwarr

Hover over offline players in the raid frame or raid window to see how long they have been offline.

Also displays a UI window with the list of players that are currently offline and how long they have been offline.

Syncs with other players that have the addon to keep timers accurate for how long someone has truly been offline, for example, if you just joined a group and there are offliners in it.
--Starts a fresh timer if no sync is available.

Show settings window: /rot
-Use sliders to size window raid members log on or off.
-Print enable/disable to show or hide some addon-related events in chat window.
-Show or hide the Raid Offline Timer window. If you closed this window with the X, uncheck and re-check this setting to bring it back.
